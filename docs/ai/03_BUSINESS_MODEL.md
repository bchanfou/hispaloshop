# Executive Summary of HispaloShop's Business Model

## 1. How Money is Made
HispaloShop generates revenue through multiple streams:
- **Transaction Fees:** A percentage taken from each transaction.
- **Subscriptions:** Users can subscribe to premium features for a monthly fee.
- **Influencer Commissions:** Compensating influencers for customer referrals.

## 2. Pricing Structure
HispaloShop offers different tiers:
- **FREE:** Basic access with limited features.
- **PRO:** €79/month for enhanced features.
- **ELITE:** €249/month for all-among features and services.

## 3. Commission Breakdown
Sellers on the platform are offered a favorable commission split as follows:
- **20%** for the basic tier.
- **18%** for the Pro tier.
- **17%** for the Elite tier.

## 4. Influencer Tiers
Influencers are categorized into the following tiers, each with distinct commission rates:
- **HÉRCULES:** 3% commission on sales.
- **ATENEA:** 5% commission on sales.
- **ZEUS:** 7% commission on sales.

## 5. First Purchase Discount
To encourage initial transactions:
- **Consumers:** Receive a 10% discount on their first purchase.
- **Platform Absorption:** The platform absorbs this discount to encourage user growth.

## 6. Attribution Period
HispaloShop operates with an **18-month attribution period**, meaning sales attributed to influencer referrals are tracked within this timeframe.

## 7. Modification of Monetization by IAs
Intelligent Assistants (IAs) have limitations on modifying monetization strategies:
- **Can Modify:** Pricing of subscriptions and transactional fees, after proper analysis.
- **Cannot Modify:** Core commission structures and discount strategies without executive approval.

## 8. Impact of Changes on Business Model
Any alterations in the monetization structure can significantly influence:
- Seller engagement and platform competitiveness.
- Influencer participation and commissions.
- Overall revenue generation and consumer perception.

By understanding these elements, HispaloShop aims to refine and optimize its business model continually, ensuring sustainable growth and a positive user experience.
